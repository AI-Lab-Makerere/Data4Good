Omulamuzi yewuunya namala
nabuuza, "Ani yakugambye bino?"
Omusajja omusiru kwekuddamu nti,
"Mukyala wange."

Omulamuzi kwekweyongerayo
okugamba nti, "Mukyala wo mugezi
nnyo, n'olwekyo ayina okuba
n'omusajja omugezi. Mujja
kuwanyisiganya abakyala." Olwo,
omulamuzi natekawo entegeka
y'okuwanyisa abakyala babwe.
