Waggoonya teyasanyuka
olw'okumuzuukusa ku makya. Anansi
n'ayonera o kugamba waggoonya nti,
"Nkusaba onnyambe, ggwe owunga
okunsinga." Waggoonya yakkiriza era
Anansi ne yeeweeka ku mugongo gwa
waggoonya okutuusa emitala
w'omugga. Olwatuuka emitala
n'abuuka mangu ku waggoonya era
n'amugamba nti, "Nkomawo mangu,
tovaawo awo!" Bw'atyo n'abulira mu
bisubi n'agenda.
