Wats okutonnya era Anansi n'awulira
ng'empewo emufuuwa nnyo ate
ng'atobye. Kye yava asalawo
okuddukira mu kituli. Kye yava
agamba muli nti, "jnenda kubeera,
mu kituli kino okutuusa enkuba
ng'ekedde. Kasita ssente zange
nazibiseeko kasooli, enkuba tezikuba."
