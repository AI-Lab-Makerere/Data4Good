Olumu ku makya Maama yampita
n'annamba nti, "Kipaku, olwaleero
owezezza emyaka mukaaga. Tulina
ekirabo kye tugenda okukuwa." Kye
nava mbuuza n'amaddu mangi nti,
"Kirabo ki? Kirabo ki?" Ko maama nti,
"Enkya bwe tunaaba tugenda mu
katale e Kampala, tujja kugenda
naawe era tujja kulinnya bbaasi."
Olw'essanyu eringi lye nalina ku
mulundi gwange ogusooka
okugendako mu kibuga, nalemwa
okwebaka ekiro ekyo.
